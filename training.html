About the Program

Welcome to our coding training program! This program is designed for both traditional learners and individuals with visual impairments, making it accessible for everyone.

  • Learning with Eyes: Traditional learning with visual aids and materials.
  • Learning without Eyes: A unique approach for individuals who wish to avoid screen-induced eye challenges or are visually impaired. You can learn coding thoroughly even if you are completely blind.
