Accessibility Matters

As a blind web developer and educator, I am deeply committed to making the web an inclusive space for everyone. My work focuses on creating web applications that prioritize usability and accessibility for individuals with disabilities, including those who rely on assistive technologies like screen readers.

By adhering to Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) and implementing best practices, I ensure that my projects meet the needs of diverse users. From semantic HTML to ARIA roles, I leverage modern tools and techniques to build experiences that empower people of all abilities.
